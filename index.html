**API Doc**

Format: baseurl/API/entity/operation/final-parameter
Endpoints for Sales:
  1. Get All Sales
    • Syntax: baseurl/API/sales/all/optional-page-number
    • Example:
      localhost/API/sales/all
      localhost/API/sales/all/3

  2. Get Total Sales By Products
    • Syntax: baseurl/API/sales/products/no-page-number
    • Example:localhost/API/sales/products

  3. Get Top 'n' Sellers
    • Syntax: baseurl/API/sales/winners/optional-number-of-winners
    • Example:
      localhost/API/sales/winners
      localhost/API/sales/winners/4

  4. Get Bottom 'n' Sellers
    • Syntax: baseurl/API/sales/losers/optional-number-of-winners
    • Example:
      localhost/API/sales/losers
      localhost/API/sales/losers/4

  5. Get Average in 'n year' Interval
    • Syntax: baseurl/API/entity/operation/compulsory-parameter
    • Example: localhost/API/entity/operation/2007-2010
      localhost/API/entity/operation/2010-2014
